About Fridge Buddy

Fridge Buddy is a web application designed to help you keep track of the food items in your fridge. It allows you to add, view, and manage your food inventory, ensuring that you never forget what you have and reducing food waste. Whether you're a busy professional or a home cook, Fridge Buddy makes it easy to stay organized and plan your meals effectively.
